Malaysia to Denmark

On 31st December 2023, I set out on a journey that many thought was impossible: cycling from Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, all the way to Denmark. Across thousands of kilometers, dozens of borders, and countless stories, this adventure became more than just a test of endurance, it was a celebration of resilience, discovery, and the kindness of strangers.

Why I Started This Journey?

To Live a Life You Will Remember

I started out just training for marathons, dragging myself through long boring runs while humming along to songs like Avicii’s The Nights. The line “One day, you’ll leave this world behind, so live a life you will remember” really hit me, and that’s where the idea of living a memorable life came from. I’ve always loved exploring — running marathons, hiking mountains, even cycling from KL to Taiping with just a backpack — but I wanted something bigger. Then one day I stumbled on the story of a Malaysian who cycled from KL to London, and it clicked: this was exactly the kind of crazy adventure I wanted. It felt achievable but not easy to do, which made it even more exciting. So I set my mind on cycling from Malaysia to the Denmark.
